We are an audiovisual production company specializing in documentaries. We like to tell committed stories with universal reach. We choose and explore knowing human realities that help us and educate us anywhere in the world, in order to give them a voice throughout the planet. We believe in good ideas over resources. We like the public to learn and at the same time to be entertained.

We seek the teaching of values, moral reflection. We communicate with coherence and honesty. Our audiovisual documentaries are critical tools for dialogue, reporting and fun.

The illusion overcomes the difficulties and as Neruda said "If you do not climb mountains, you will never be able to enjoy the landscape".
